UL+

Logo

UL+ is a university group which aims to help young entrepreneurs to start their own business. This logo was created by me and another designer. We collaborated to design the logo and manage the brainstorming to achieve UL+ name. For the logo we used bright colors hoping to easily connect with youth spirit and energy. We also used a sans-serif font with boldness to strike out the the potential audience.
