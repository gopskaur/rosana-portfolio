Baía das Gatas Festival

Posters

Every year in an exotic bay there is a famous Music Festival in Cape Verde which takes place; currently called Baía das Gatas. This Festival of african music happens on the São Vicente Island. In a university project I was asked to choose an event and create three different posters to promote it. Since I have african roots, I thought this would be a great chance to explore the african graphic language and bring the surface to my influences. Africa is a continent full of rhythm and colours which I wanted to represent in this project; where we can feel music through the shapes and colors used, I hope to have encaptured these feelings within this particular project.
