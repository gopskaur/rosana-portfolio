Cais

Posters, Magazine

Cais is a magazine inspired on the english magazine The Big Issue which is sold to help people with social and economic needs. Cais magazine has articles about culture, science and many others. In a University project we had to create a magazine with a subject selected by our work group and also develop promotion materials. My group choose to write about different types of arts so we choose one person of each of the follow themes: architecture, product design, music and graphic design. To promote our magazine we created an event with conferences about each type of art and posters adapted to each theme and day.


One of the chapters within the Cais magazine has the theme of music. Black and white are two predominant colors used throughout this design, whilst presenting the rhythm of music through circles and lines to give the design a use of flow.
