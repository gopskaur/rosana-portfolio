Wagglies

Packaging

Wagglies is a online shop for dogs suplies. One of the selling products is nail clippers. I was asked to create a packaging with a fresh design which could stand out from other competitors in line with the brand colors and design. To send to each client who buys the nails clippers they will also have an free ebook to teach how to trim your dog's nails.


One of the selling products are probiotics for Dogs. I was also asked to create a packaging design which could potentially stand out from other competitors.
